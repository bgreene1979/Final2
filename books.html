Spirit of the Forest

Published by Xlibris Publishing

 Romelia was abandoned as a baby and left for dead. But a nearby deer soon changed her fate. She was raised by the deer in a forest untouched by humans and time.
 Cyri’s parents never cared for him. They wanted a girl, but his twin died in a freak accident. Haunted by her memory, they shunned him until he left to join one of the land’s feared gangs.
 When Romelia finds Cyri unconscious and gravely injured, her small forest paradise is soon expanded into a whole world as he tells her of the world and his travels.
 But Cyri’s old gang is still looking for him, and a freak fire alerts the world that Romelia’s paradise is not as abandoned as it seems. With Romelia sick, Cyri is the only one who can stop the danger that is coming from all sides. And more importantly, stop Dominique from kidnapping Romelia.
